During my internship, I had the opportunity to attend two events. Each event was a memorable experience that provided valuable new insights, exciting gifts, and wonderful moments.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)
&emsp;**Event Title:** Exploring and Updating Cloud Computing Technology Trends with AWS Experts

&emsp;**Time:** 09:00 AM, May 9, 2025

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Your Role:** Attendee

&emsp;**Brief Description of Content and Key Activities:**
* Listened to AWS experts share the latest optimized services and features in the AWS ecosystem, focusing on cloud cost management, infrastructure security, and system performance optimization.
* Explored the integration trends of Generative AI into cloud platforms (such as using AWS Bedrock) to build intelligent applications efficiently.
* Gained technical insights into optimizing Microservices architecture, identity and access management (IAM/Cognito), and industry best practices for deploying highly stable and secure systems in production environments.

&emsp;**Key Takeaways and Value Gained:** Acquired a solid structural mindset for designing cloud-native systems, understood the critical importance of infrastructure cost optimization, and caught up with advanced AI technologies driving the IT industry.


### [Event 2](4.2-Event2/)
&emsp;**Event Title:** Career Orientation in the Tech Industry: Strategic Insights from Leadership

&emsp;**Time:** 09:00 AM, June 06, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Your Role:** Attendee

&emsp;**Brief Description of Content and Key Activities:**
* Gained a panoramic view from the CEO regarding market shifts in the IT industry, the intersection between Tech and Business operations, and the challenges and opportunities for IT talent in the AI era.
* Developed a Product Mindset, learning that a proficient engineer should not just focus on writing clean code but must understand how the product solves user pain points and delivers economic value to the business.
* Discussed the core requirements for young professionals entering the tech workforce, including self-learning capabilities, critical thinking, teamwork, communication skills, and technological agility.

&emsp;**Key Takeaways and Value Gained:** Shifted from a purely technical perspective to a broader business-oriented viewpoint; clearly recognized the essential soft skills and self-management capabilities needed to prepare a solid foundation for entering the competitive labor market.
