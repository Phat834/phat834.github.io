Topic Name: Cloud Architect Competition and AWS Security, Monitoring, & Certification Solutions.

**Key Highlights Summary:**

Securing Web Apps with AWS Security Agent (Speaker - Mr. Thinh): This session delved into utilizing automated agents for penetration testing (Automated Pentesting). The speaker analyzed the operational pricing reality and pointed out critical limitations of current agents: being stopped in their tracks by authentication blocks (MFA, Biometrics, mTLS), struggling to detect business-logic flaws and fraud without deep context, and rapid task-hour accumulation that makes continuous monitoring mandatory. The topic concluded with a practical live demo.

System Monitoring within Risk Management (Speaker - Mr. Son): The content focused on defining SLA (Service Level Agreement) and the strategic positioning of monitoring inside overall risk management. The speaker highlighted the clear gap between a "healthy infrastructure" and "happy users", and introduced the Monitoring Pyramid to help build a comprehensive and effective tracking and alerting system.

Inside the Exam: AWS Cloud Practitioner (Speaker - Mr. Huy): This presentation provided an insider look into the foundational AWS Cloud Practitioner certification exam. The speaker introduced the exam structure, the required knowledge levels, learning pathways, and structural approaches to mastering AWS cloud computing concepts for beginners.

Key Takeaways (Knowledge/Skills): Gained a realistic understanding of the potentials and constraints of automated security agents on AWS; mastered the mindset of building a user-centric monitoring system rather than just focusing on infrastructure metrics; acquired a clear roadmap and methodology to confidently prepare for and pass the AWS Cloud Practitioner exam.
