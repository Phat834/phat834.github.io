Topic Title: Career Orientation in the Tech Industry and Strategic Perspectives from a Leader.

**Key Summary:**

The Big Picture of the IT Industry: The CEO shared insights into current shifts in the tech market and the intersection between technology (Tech) and business operations (Business). It also highlighted the challenges and opportunities for IT personnel amid the boom of AI.

Product Mindset: Emphasis was placed on the idea that a good engineer does not just write correct and clean code, but must also understand what problem their product solves for users and what economic value it brings to the enterprise.

Requirements for Young Talents: Sharing the core skills that tech companies look for in fresh graduates: Self-learning ability, critical thinking, teamwork, communication, and adaptability to new technologies.

Key Takeaways (Knowledge/Skills): Shifting perspective from a purely technical standpoint to a broader business vision; clearly identifying soft skills that need cultivation to prepare for entering the actual labor market.
