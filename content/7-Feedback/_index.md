General Evaluation
1. Working Environment
The workplace atmosphere here is exceptionally dynamic and flexible. Whether working on-site or remotely (WFH), I always received prompt and enthusiastic support from colleagues whenever technical bottlenecks arose. The office space is modern, well-organized, and sufficiently quiet, which perfectly fosters deep focus. If there is one minor recommendation, I hope the company can host more networking events and increase the office-attendance quota so that interns can build stronger professional bonds.

2. Mentor / Team Admin Support
I am deeply impressed by the structured and dedicated support from both the Admin team and Mentors. The Admins handle paperwork and operations seamlessly, ensuring information and assets are delivered efficiently. Regarding mentorship, the approach is highly progressive; instead of handing out immediate solutions, my mentor guided me through thought-provoking questions, encouraging independent troubleshooting. This high degree of autonomy in pacing my learning path allowed me to maximize my self-research capabilities.

3. Alignment Between Work and Academic Major
Initially, I assumed that Cloud infrastructure might be somewhat detached from my core Information Technology curriculum. However, diving deep into configuration made me realize that Cloud is the definitive backbone of modern software architecture. Mastering Cloud-Native concepts, Serverless patterns, and high-level security permissions not only reinforced my backend development foundations but also broadened my tech horizon far beyond traditional university lectures.

4. Learning & Development Opportunities
This internship has provided me with an immense volume of practical knowledge. Transitioning from theoretical understanding, I am now capable of architecting a complete Cloud environment for a live project from scratch. This "learning-by-doing" methodology accelerated my professional growth significantly. Furthermore, observing the daily workflows of industry experts helped me map out a clear trajectory for my future career.

5. Corporate Culture & Team Spirit
The organization maintains a perfect equilibrium between a friendly, approachable atmosphere and absolute professionalism when executing tasks. Our team spirit was exemplified by how our 5-member team structured and delegated tasks based on individual strengths. Every member's strong accountability, paired with mutual support from experienced colleagues, made me feel like an integral part of a real engineering team.

6. Policies / Benefits for Interns
The company offers flexible scheduling, allowing me to maintain a healthy balance between my university commitments and internship tasks. The training documentation is highly comprehensive. Notably, gaining access to specialized Tech Talks and internal technology events was a massive value-add that sparked numerous innovative ideas for my projects.

### Additional Questions
What did you find most satisfying during your internship?
The privilege of getting hands-on experience with cutting-edge Cloud services within a global-standard workspace, guided by highly supportive mentors and admins.

What areas do you think the company should improve for future interns?
Optimizing the office seating registration system to allow more on-site working days. Additionally, the internal video tutorials should be audited and refreshed since the AWS Console UI and certain service features have undergone significant updates compared to the legacy documentation.

If recommending to a friend, would you encourage them to intern here? Why?
Absolutely YES. It is a premier tech launchpad with high practical relevance, enabling students to build a robust professional profile and mindset essential for thriving in the IT industry.

### Proposals & Aspirations
* **Do you have any proposals to improve the internship experience?**
Updating and synchronizing the internal labs and guides with the latest AWS releases. Overall, the entire journey has been outstanding.

* **Would you like to continue with this program in the future?**
Yes, I am highly receptive to future opportunities.
